Kim's Lasagna

Description:

Everyone raves about this lasagna just the way it is,although I'll probably
never stop tweaking it!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bulk italian sausage
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 4 cloves garlic,minced
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 2(8 ounce)cans tomato sauce
  • 1(14 ounce)can crushed tomatoes
  • 1(14 ounce)can italian-style crushed tomatoes
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoons salt

Steps:

  1. Brown sausage and ground beef with onion and garlic in a large Dutch oven or heavy pot over medium heat,cooking and stirring until meat is cooked through, 10 to 15 minutes
  2. Meanwhile,place lasagna noodles into a deep bowl and cover with very hot trap water;let soak for 30 minutes
  3. Beat egg in a bowl and stir ricotta cheese, 2 tablespoons parsley,1/2 teaspoon salt, and nutmeg into egg until thoroughly combined
  4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  5. Cover bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish with 1 cup sauce.
  6. Bake until lasagna noodles are tender and casserole is bubbling, about 45 minutes.
