ShortBread Cookies

Description:

This classic shortbread cookie recipe makes rich, buttery, melt-in-your-mouth press cookies using just 4 simple
ingredients that you probably have in your pantry.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups butter,softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 4 cups all-purpose

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  3. Beat softened butter and sugar together in a large bowl with an electric mixer
  4. Stir in vanilla;add flour and mix well until combined
  5. Fill cookie press with dough and form cookies onto two ungreased cookie sheets, spacing them about 1 inches apart
